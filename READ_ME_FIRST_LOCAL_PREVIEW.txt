OHGS WEBSITE - LOCAL PREVIEW INSTRUCTIONS

IMPORTANT:
Do not open the website directly inside the ZIP preview.
First extract/unzip this folder.

ON PHONE:
1. Open Files / My Files.
2. Find this ZIP in Downloads.
3. Tap and hold it, then choose Extract / Unzip.
4. Open the extracted folder.
5. Tap index.html.
6. Choose Opera or Chrome.

ON COMPUTER:
1. Right click ZIP.
2. Extract All.
3. Open the extracted folder.
4. Double click index.html.

BEST LOCAL TEST:
If images or styles do not load on phone, upload the full extracted folder to GitHub, then view from Vercel.
Phone file previews sometimes block linked CSS, JS, images, and videos.
